Newton's Laws of Motion

First Law: Law of Inertia

Objects at rest stay at rest, and objects in motion stay in motion with the same speed and in the same direction unless acted upon by an unbalanced external force.

Second Law: Force and Acceleration

The acceleration of an object is directly proportional to the net force acting upon the object and inversely proportional to the object's mass.

Third Law: Action and Reaction

For every action, there is an equal and opposite reaction.
